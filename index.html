Cognitive Architecture

Five Lenses of Thought

Each stream applies a distinct cognitive lens, ensuring no dimension of reasoning is overlooked.

Analytical

Logic, evidence, and data-driven reasoning. Breaks problems into measurable components and identifies quantifiable patterns.

Creative

Novel connections, lateral thinking, and analogies. Explores unconventional solutions and reframes problems from surprising angles.

Critical

Devil's advocate, risks, and counterarguments. Stress-tests assumptions and identifies blind spots others miss.

Systems

Interconnections, feedback loops, and emergence. Maps complex dependencies and identifies leverage points within larger systems.

Pragmatic

Feasibility, implementation, and constraints. Grounds ideas in real-world timelines, budgets, and actionable next steps.
